Popular Tours & Excursions

Join our guided tours to explore the hidden wonders of Dinagat.
From pristine coves to mystic lakes, there's always something to discover.

Island Hopping Adventures

Discover white sand beaches, hidden lagoons, and towering karst cliffs
aboard traditional motorized outrigger boats.

Lake Bababu Trek

Hike through dense forests to reach the mystical meromictic lake

Cultural Tours

Visit the PBMA Shrine and the Founder's Monument,

Activities, Delicacies & Accommodations

Experience the best adventures and tastes Dinagat Islands has to offer.

Island Hopping

Visit famous destinations like Bitaog Beach, Cab-ilan Island, and Pangabangan Island with expert local boatmen.

Exploring Lake Bababu

Trek through the lush landscape to swim in the mystical meromictic lake, known for its unique underwater cave.

Diving & Snorkeling

Discover rich coral reefs and vibrant marine life in the clear waters surrounding the islands of the province.

Local Delicacies

Feast on fresh seafood, try the unique "saang" (spider conch), and bring home locally dried fish delicacies.

Cultural Immersion

Explore the unique heritage of Dinagat by visiting the grand PBMA Shrine overlooking the serene San Jose Town.

Accommodations

Enjoy comfortable stays varying from budget-friendly local inns and homestays to relaxing beach resorts in San Jose and Basilisa.

About Dinagat Islands

Dinagat Islands is a province in the Caraga region of the Philippines, known for its pristine beaches, beautiful karst landscapes, and laid-back island vibes.
