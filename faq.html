How to get to Dinagat Islands?

The most accessible route is via Surigao City. From Surigao City Boulevard or Pantalan 2, take a passenger boat (RoRo or fast craft) to San Jose, Dinagat Islands. The ferry ride takes approximately 1 to 1.5 hours. Surigao City can be reached via direct flights from Manila or Cebu.

What is the best time to visit?

The best time to visit Dinagat Islands is during the dry season, from March to October. During this period, the waters are calmer, making island hopping and engaging in water activities much safer and more enjoyable.

Are there banks or ATMs on the island?

Yes, there are a few ATMs in the capital town of San Jose. However, it is highly recommended to bring enough cash for your entire trip, especially when visiting remote beaches and neighboring municipalities where cash is often the only accepted means of payment.
